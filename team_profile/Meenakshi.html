About me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate working on Intelligent Robotics at the Dept. of Aerospace Engineering, Indian Institute of Science (IISc Bangalore).

I have collaborated with Prof. Evangelos Theodorou from Georgia Institute of Technology, USA and Prof. Raghu Krishnapuram at the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber Physical Systems (RBCCPS). During my Ph.D. I've spent time at TCS Research (Innovation Labs) as a Summer Research Intern.

The primary focus of my research is on developing safe vision based navigation algorithms for mobile platforms. Prior to joining IISc, I received my masters in Mechatronics from CSIR-Central Mechanical Engineering Research Institute, Durgapur, India. I am a recipient of Gandhian Young Technological Award 2015 and INAE National Best MTech Thesis Award 2015.
