Aditya Hegde

About me

Aditya Hegde is pursuing his PhD with a specialization in multi-agent systems, cooperative and distributed control. He received his M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a specialization in Systems and Control, and his B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Delhi, New Delhi. Prior to joining the PhD program, he was working at Cummins Inc. in Columbus, Indiana as a Senior Systems Integration Engineer.
