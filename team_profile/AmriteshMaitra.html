About me

I have been broadly working on control, guidance, and strategy aspects in Multi-Agent Systems. My interests also involve bio-inspired strategies relevant to robotics and unmanned vehicle missions. I am an Electrical Engineer by qualification (Bachelor of Engineering, Jadavpur University) and have a post-graduate degree in Control Systems Engineering (Master of Technology, IIT Kharagpur). I had also worked in manufacturing industry for 13 months (Hot Strip Mill, JSL Stainles Ltd, Jajpur, India) and have experience in Control and Aautimation Softwares, Sensor Systems, Power System Protection schemes, and various aspects of electrical distribution and utilization of a manufacturing plant.
